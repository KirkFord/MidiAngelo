Our Journey so Far

Making music from drawing

You can draw what you want on the canvas and can listen to your art. Our website does some sorcery to convert your drawing to music!

Choosing instruments that play over the .midi file

With a library of instruments for you to select from, you can choose the sounds that create your music. It's like your very own band!

A big drawing canvas is your playground

Draw whatever you can imagine on the canvas provided. Feel the canvas is too small? No problem! Increase the canvas size as you see fit.

Choose your tempo

You have made your song but feel the track is either slow or fast? Don't worry. Change the pace of your music with the playback speed modifier built into the audio player!

Create an Account and Stay ahead

We have big features planned for our users with accounts, plus, signup is free and easy!

Check out our Roadmap!

User Galleries

See the sights and sounds of what you've created and explore the imaginations of other users!

Organize contests

Host competitions to see who comes up with the best art and music by setting up a leaderboard and letting people vote on the submissions.

Know where you stand in your community

See the contests users participated and where you're placed among them.

Comment your thoughts on others work

Want to have a voice? Give feedback on someone's creation by commenting your thoughts for the artist to see!

Works in Progress

The Mona Lisa wasn't made in a day, so users should be able to save their work periodically letting you finish that masterpiece later.
